Video game sales analysis

Video game sales analysis Analyzing video game sales from year 1980 to 2016. Dataset Dataset was acquired from https://www.kaggle.com/gregorut/videogamesales. This dataset contains a list of video games with sales greater than 100,000 copies. It was generated by a scrape of vgchartz.com.

The following task were carried out in this project:

  • Data cleaning
  • Who are the top 25 Publishers by sales?
  • What are the top 25 Platforms by sales?
  • Top 3 Publishers and their sales over the years
  • What genre made most sales?
  • Which year was most sales made?

World COVID19 Data Exploration

COVID19 is a virus that is rapidly growing. In a fast-evolving pandemic it is not a simple matter to identify the countries that are most successful in making progress against it. For a comprehensive assessment, we track the impact of the pandemic across our publication and we built country profiles for 207 countries to study in depth the statistics on the coronavirus pandemic for every country in the world.
Link to Dataset: https://ourworldindata.org/covid-deaths

Objectives:

  • Data cleaning
  • Which countries have the highest infection rate compared to population
  • Which countries have the highest death rate compared to population
  • Which continents have the highest death rate compared to population
  • What is the cumulative number of confirmed deaths?
